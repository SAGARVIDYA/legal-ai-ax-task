GST Registration is a process through which businesses obtain a Goods and Services Tax Identification Number (GSTIN) under India's GST system.

Businesses whose turnover exceeds the prescribed threshold limit must register under GST. Registration enables businesses to collect GST from customers and claim input tax credits.

GST registration can be completed online through the GST portal by submitting business details, identity proof, address proof, and other required documents.

Failure to register when required may result in penalties and legal consequences. GST registration helps businesses comply with tax laws and operate legally.
