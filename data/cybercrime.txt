Cyber crime refers to illegal activities carried out using computers, networks, or the internet. Common cyber crimes include hacking, identity theft, online fraud, phishing, cyber bullying, and data theft.

Cyber laws in India provide legal provisions to prevent and punish cyber offences. Victims of cyber crime can report incidents to cyber crime cells and online reporting portals.

Penalties for cyber crimes may include fines, imprisonment, or both depending on the nature and severity of the offence.
